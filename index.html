About Me

Building real things,
in public

I believe the fastest way to learn is to ship publicly — and I've done exactly that.

I'm a web development and graphic design student at As Sa'adah Institute who doesn't wait for permission to build. I've designed and deployed 14+ websites using AI-assisted workflows and Canva — managing everything from concept to live deployment.

My toolkit spans front-end development with HTML, CSS, and AI-assisted workflows, visual design and branding using Canva and graphic design principles, and digital marketing fundamentals applied to real client and personal projects. I'm currently progressing through freeCodeCamp's CSS curriculum — up to Pseudo-Selectors — and picking up Python on the side.

I document my learning publicly — the wins, the pivots, and the builds. That means you can literally see my thinking and work quality evolve in real time. Transparency isn't just a value; it's my portfolio.

Currently open to freelance web design or digital marketing projects, internship or mentorship opportunities, and collaborative student projects in tech or design. If you're building something and want a hands-on, fast-learning collaborator, let's talk.
